Why Abundant
The problem I care about
At CampusX, most of my model failures came down to training signal quality — not architecture. Abundant is working on that problem at frontier scale. That's a real bottleneck, not a buzzword.
Why RL data specifically
The next capability gains won't come from scraping the web. They'll come from simulation and learning from experience. That's where Abundant sits — and it's genuinely early.
Why Research Fellow vs pure engineering
I've never just built infrastructure — I've also had to define what "good" looks like, run experiments, and close the loop. The RPM role is exactly that intersection.
The environment I do best in
Open-ended, high-ownership work where the answer isn't handed to you. I've built production ML without a playbook before — that's when I'm sharpest.
